Privacy Policy & Terms of Analysis

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Acuity Narrative Integrity Engine ("Acuity," "A.N.I.E.," "we," "us," or "our") is a forensic analysis tool designed to evaluate public web content. By installing or using the Acuity Chrome Extension, Mobile Application, or Web Scanner, you ("User") agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To function, Acuity collects specific data points. We categorize these strictly:

A. Voluntarily Submitted Content (The "Scan")

When you explicitly click the "Scan" function, you grant us permission to access, process, and store the URL and text content of the active browser tab or input. This is not background collection; it is "on-demand" data submission.

B. Technical & Device Data

We collect your IP address, browser type, and a randomly generated anonymous Installation ID ("Ghost ID"). This is used solely for security, rate-limiting, and abuse prevention.

C. Analysis Data

We generate and store proprietary metadata derived from your Scans, including but not limited to: Forensic Scores, Rhetorical Vector Analysis, and verification logs ("Analysis Data").

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To generate the Narrative Integrity Score via our cloud infrastructure.
  • System Improvement: To calibrate our linguistic models and caching algorithms.
  • Aggregated Intelligence (Our Right): We reserve the right to aggregate, anonymize, and analyze Scan Data to identify global misinformation trends. We retain full ownership rights over all aggregated, non-personally identifiable Analysis Data and may use, publish, or monetize this aggregated dataset for strategic research or commercial purposes.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) to third parties. However, to provide the service, data must be processed by trusted infrastructure providers:

  • Render & Supabase: For secure cloud hosting and database storage.
  • LLM Providers (e.g., xAI, OpenAI): Text content is sent to these providers solely for processing. We do not allow these providers to use your data to train their public models.
  • Search Partners (e.g., Tavily, Firecrawl): Search queries derived from the text are sent to verify claims.

5. Data Retention

We retain Analysis Data (URLs and Scores) indefinitely to maintain our global integrity cache. We do not retain granular browsing history. We only retain the specific URLs you chose to scan.
